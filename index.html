Mandatory Document Structure

Formatting: Times New Roman. Main Text: 12pt, Sub-Topics: 14pt (Bold), Titles: 16pt (Bold).

  1. Front Page (Name, Enrollment, Course Details)
  2. Second Title Page (Outline of ethnographies on [Book 1] & [Book 2] - Tentative)
  3. Contents Page
  4. Ethnography 1 Review:
    • Abstract: Max 300 words.
    • Introduction: Research Question (1 para), Objectives (must start with "To..."), Significance (point-wise, half page).
    • Material and Methods: Study Area (describe terrain/forest), Population (explicitly state sample size and age), Data Types (qualitative/quantitative specifics), Collection Methods (interviews, observation, genealogy, etc.), Data Analysis (Descriptive/Analytical).
    • Key Findings: Point-wise, Min. 2 pages.
    • Comparison with Contemporary Context: Point-wise, Min. 1.5 pages. Compare with articles from the last 10 years.
    • Summary: Synthesizing this specific text.
  5. Ethnography 2 Review: (Follow exact same structure as Ethnography 1 above).
  6. References: A single, combined bibliography at the very end.
